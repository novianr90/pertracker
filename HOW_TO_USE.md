# How to Use PerTracker

Welcome to PerTracker! This guide will walk you through the core modules of the app to help you start managing your finances effectively.

## 0. Initial Setup (Optional)
Check the [`HOW_TO_SETUP.md`](HOW_TO_SETUP.md) file for instructions on how to configure the app to automatically sync your transaction data to Google Sheets.

---

## 1. Categories
*Categories are a one-time setup (unless you uninstall the app or clear local data).*

1. Tap the **Categories** button on the Dashboard.
2. Tap the floating action button (`+` icon) at the bottom right corner.
3. Enter the **Category Name**.
4. Select the category type based on its function:
   * **[Income]**: For earning transactions (e.g., Salary, Bonus).
   * **[Expense]**: For spending transactions (e.g., Food, Transport).
   * **[Goal Transfer]**: For money set aside for a specific financial goal (e.g., Saving for Electronics). Goal transfers act similarly as expenses by reducing your active disposable cash, but they safely allocate funds into your Goal's progression.
5. Tap **Save**.

---

## 2. Budget
*Budgets are set up on a monthly basis. You must create or set your budget targets every time a new month arrives.*

1. Tap the **Budget** button on the Dashboard.
2. Tap the floating action button (`+` icon).
3. Select a **Category** from the dropdown (this pulls from the categories you added in step 1).
4. Define the **Month** and **Year** for this specific budget.
5. Enter the **Budget Amount**. This number will serve as your *`Planned Amount`* limit for that category during the specified time frame.
6. Tap **Save**.

---

## 3. Daily Transactions
*Record your everyday income, expenses, and goal transfers here to keep your budgets updated.*

1. On the main **Dashboard**, tap the prominent floating action button (`+` icon).
2. Select the relevant **Category**, enter the **Amount**, and add any **Remarks** (notes).
3. Select the **Date** of the transaction (it defaults to today's date).
4. Tap **Save Transaction**.

---

## 4. Goals
*Goals help you track and allocate money that you set aside using the "Goal Transfer" categories.*

1. Tap the **Goals** button on the Dashboard.
2. Tap the floating action button (`+` icon).
3. Enter the **Goal Title** and the **Target Amount** you wish to achieve.
4. Tap **Save**.

> **Note**: Whenever you record a daily transaction under a category typed as *Goal Transfer*, you will be prompted to select your active Goal to securely store and link those transactions!

---

## 5. Portfolio
*The portfolio page acts as a ledger to log and monitor the assets/wealth you currently hold. (Note: More advanced updates will be coming to this page).*

1. Tap the **Portfolio** button on the Dashboard.
2. Tap the floating action button (`+` icon).
3. Enter the **Asset Name**.
4. Specify whether the asset is **Syariah** compliant.
5. Enter the **Total Units** purchased, the **Average Price**, and the **Current Market Price**.
6. Tap **Save**.
